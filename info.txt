## Suggested Tech Stack

### Backend

* **Python + FastAPI** (high performance and easy to integrate with your existing Python MT5 code)

### Database

* **PostgreSQL**

### ORM

* SQLAlchemy

### Background Jobs

* Celery + Redis (best for scaling) or APScheduler (simpler for a smaller deployment)

### Frontend

* **Next.js (React)** for both Admin and Client dashboards

### Charts

* Recharts or Chart.js

### Authentication

* JWT + Refresh Tokens

### Deployment

* AWS EC2 (where your MT5 bot can already run)
* Nginx
* Docker
* HTTPS with Let's Encrypt

## Long-Term Vision

Think of this as a **"Trading Fund Management Platform"** rather than just a bot. Once this foundation is in place, adding features becomes much easier:

* Multiple MT5 accounts per client
* Investor-specific fee structures
* Stripe or Razorpay alongside crypto payments
* Referral and affiliate tracking
* KYC/document uploads
* Support ticket system
* Mobile app (using the same FastAPI backend)
* White-label support for other traders or firms

Designing the backend as modular services from the beginning will let you keep extending the platform without major rewrites.
